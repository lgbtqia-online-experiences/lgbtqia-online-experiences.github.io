Welcome

Welcome to this research project on the online experiences of LGBTQIA+ people.

You may already have completed the first part of this project, which was the survey. If you have not yet completed it, you can do so here: Participate in the survey.

If you signed up for the next stage, this website will guide you through the data donation component of the project.

Participation in social media data donation helps us build a more complete and meaningful understanding of LGBTQIA+ experiences.
