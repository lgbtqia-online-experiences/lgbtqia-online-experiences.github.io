This page provides guidance for participants who wish to donate **Youtube data**.

## Before you start

Before beginning, please make sure you have:
- completed the survey or signed up for the study
- read the information on the Data Donation page
- access to your YouTube account

## How it works

You will be guided through the process of accessing and exporting your data from Youtube.
